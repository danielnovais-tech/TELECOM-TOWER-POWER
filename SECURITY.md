# Security Policy

We take the security of TELECOM TOWER POWER seriously. Thank you for helping us keep our customers and their data safe.

## Reporting a vulnerability

**Please do not open public GitHub issues for security problems.**

Email **[security@telecomtowerpower.com.br](mailto:security@telecomtowerpower.com.br)** with:

1. A clear description of the vulnerability and affected component (API endpoint, repo file, third-party integration, etc.).
2. Steps to reproduce, including any required preconditions (account tier, region, headers).
3. Proof-of-concept code, screenshots, or HTTP traces.
4. Your assessment of impact (data exposure, RCE, privilege escalation, denial of service…).
5. Whether you would like public credit if we publish a post-mortem.

If you need to encrypt the report, request our PGP key in your first message.

## Our commitment

| | |
|---|---|
| **Acknowledgement** | within **72 hours** |
| **Triage and severity assignment** | within **5 business days** |
| **Status updates** | at least every **7 days** until resolved |
| **Coordinated disclosure window** | up to **90 days** from acknowledgement; can be extended for complex fixes by mutual agreement |

We will not pursue legal action against researchers who:

- Make a good-faith effort to avoid privacy violations, data destruction, and service interruption.
- Give us reasonable time to remediate before any public disclosure.
- Do not exfiltrate more data than necessary to demonstrate the issue.
- Do not exploit the vulnerability beyond what is required for the report.

## Scope

In scope:

- `*.telecomtowerpower.com.br` (api, app, docs, monitoring)
- This GitHub repository (`danielnovaisantunes-tech/TELECOM-TOWER-POWER`)
- Published Python and JavaScript SDKs
- Stripe webhook handler and signed payload validation

Out of scope (please do not test):

- Third-party services (AWS, Stripe, Cloudflare, Anthropic) — report directly to the vendor.
- Social engineering, phishing, or physical attacks against our team or office.
- DoS / volumetric attacks. We have rate limiting; please do not stress it.
- Theoretical issues without a working PoC (e.g. CSP minor warnings, missing headers on static assets).
- Reports auto-generated by scanners with no analyst follow-up.

## Hall of fame

We publicly thank confirmed reporters at the bottom of `docs-site/docs/legal/security-credits.md` after the issue is fixed and the disclosure window passes (unless you prefer to remain anonymous).

## Bug bounty

We do not currently run a paid bounty programme. For impactful, well-written reports we may, at our discretion, send a one-off thank-you (swag or a token of appreciation). This is **not** a contractual obligation.

Available on request from `security@telecomtowerpower.com.br` (key ID published on the website footer once finalised).
