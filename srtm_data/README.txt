Place SRTM .hgt tiles here (e.g. S16W048.hgt)

Download tiles from:
  https://dds.cr.usgs.gov/srtm/version2_1/SRTM3/

For the Brasília region (towers_brazil.csv), download:
  S16W048.hgt  — covers lat -16 to -15, lon -48 to -47

Naming convention:
  N/S + abs(floor(lat)), E/W + abs(floor(lon))
  e.g. latitude -15.78, longitude -47.93 → S16W048.hgt
